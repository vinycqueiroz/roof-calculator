About Us

This calculator was built by roofers, for roofers. With years of hands-on experience in framing, finish carpentry, and roofing projects; We know how important it is to save time on the job while keeping estimates accurate.

Our goal is to save time and cut costs in the process of estimating a roof, and when calculating roof size; whether you’re a professional contractor, an architect or tackling your own roofing project. We’re passionate about creating tools that help people work smarter and quicker, not harder.

We will be working on new upcoming features to make roof calculating easier, any recommendations you might have be sure to email us!
