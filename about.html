About Us

Born in Brazil and raised in the United States, I always had a passion for building things — whether with my hands or with code. I attended an information technology high school where I got my first taste of programming, but at the time I didn't have the patience to stick with it.

Years later, at 21, I decided to give it another shot — and this time it clicked. My first real project was a website I built to help with my day-to-day work: calculating roofing materials for a mid-sized construction company. What started as a personal tool quickly became something I'm genuinely proud of.

This is Roofing 101 — built from real experience, for real people in the trade. I hope it saves you as much time as it saves me.

Built by roofers, for roofers.
